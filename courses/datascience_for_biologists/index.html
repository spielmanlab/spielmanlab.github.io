This course meets remotely Monday and Thursday 2-3:15 pm and Wednesday 3:30-6:15 pm. Zoom links can be found in Canvas. All grading and assignment submissions (unless otherwise stated) will be hosted on Canvas, but course materials will be posted on this site and/or within the rstudio.cloud BIOL01301 workspace.
